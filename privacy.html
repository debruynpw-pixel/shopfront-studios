3. Legal basis for processing

Under POPIA we process your information on these grounds:

  • Consent — you chose to fill in the quote form or message us.
  • Legitimate interest — understanding how the site is used so we can improve it, and keeping records of enquiries and quotes.
  • Contractual necessity — if you become a client, we need your details to deliver the work and invoice you.

4. Cookies and analytics

This site uses cookies and similar technologies for two purposes:

  • Essential — remembering your progress through the quote flow so you do not lose your answers. These cannot be switched off without breaking the site.
  • Analytics and advertising — Google Analytics and Google Ads tags, which tell us how many people visited a page and whether an advert led to an enquiry. This data is aggregated and does not identify you by name.

You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings at any time. Doing so may break the quote flow but the rest of the site will work normally. You can also opt out of Google Analytics using Google's opt-out browser add-on.

5. Who we share your information with

We do not sell your personal information. We have never done so and we do not intend to.

We share it only with service providers who help us run the business, and only to the extent they need it:

  • Vercel — website hosting and delivery.
  • Google — Analytics and Ads measurement.
  • Meta (WhatsApp) — if you choose to contact us via WhatsApp, that conversation is subject to WhatsApp's own privacy terms.
  • FormSubmit — delivers quote form submissions to our inbox.
  • Email provider — to receive and reply to your enquiry.

Some of these providers process data outside South Africa. Where that happens, we rely on the provider's own contractual safeguards, as permitted under section 72 of POPIA.

We may also disclose information if the law requires it, such as a court order or a valid request from a regulator.

6. How long we keep it

  • Enquiries that do not become projects: up to 24 months, then deleted.
  • Client records: for the duration of the project and then as long as tax and company law requires, which is generally five years.
  • Analytics data: retained according to Google's default retention settings.

7. How we protect it

We take reasonable technical and organisational steps to keep your information secure: the site is served over HTTPS, access to enquiry data is limited to people who need it, and accounts are protected with strong authentication.

No system is perfectly secure, and we will not pretend otherwise. If a breach occurs that creates a real risk to you, we will notify you and the Information Regulator as POPIA requires.

8. Your rights

Under POPIA you have the right to:

  • Ask what personal information we hold about you.
  • Ask us to correct information that is wrong or incomplete.
  • Ask us to delete information we no longer have a lawful reason to keep.
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Withdraw consent at any time, without affecting processing that already happened.
  • Complain to the Information Regulator if you believe we have mishandled your information.

To exercise any of these, email debruynpw@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days. There is no charge for a reasonable request.
